About

W A V E P L A Z A   is an open vaporwave library powered by archive.org.


Distributing


If you want to distribute to our library follow the following steps:

  • Upload your music (preferably in MP3 format, .flac is also supported) to archive.org.
  • Your repository must contain a cover_thumb.jpg file
  • Your repository must have valid metadata for artist, album and track title.
  • Make sure you upload your album in "Community Audio". This way the website will take care of the metadata
  • Send me a link to the repository by sending me and email: email@email.com
